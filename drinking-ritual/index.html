The 3-Minute Bedtime Ritual (What I Did)

I didn't quit drinking through force.

I quit by reprogramming my subconscious—in 3 minutes every night before bed.

Here's the exact ritual:

Step 1: Awareness (30 seconds)

Notice what you're feeling right now. Not thinking—feeling. Where is it in your body? What does it feel like? Just observe.

Step 2: Identity Shift (60 seconds)

Ask: "Who is the version of me that doesn't need alcohol?" Feel what that person feels. Not visualize—feel. Calm. Grounded. Free.

Step 3: Nurture the Feeling (90 seconds)

Sit with that feeling. Breathe it in. Let it settle into your nervous system. Don't force it. Just allow it.

That's it.

3 minutes. Every night. Before bed.

And within weeks, something shifted.

The cravings weakened. Then disappeared.

Not because I was white-knuckling it.

Because I became someone who didn't want alcohol anymore.
